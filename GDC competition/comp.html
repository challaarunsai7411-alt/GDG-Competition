Welcome to Google Developer Groups

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are local communities for developers interested in learning, sharing, and connecting with others about technology.
All are welcome, from beginners to experienced professionals, to join hands-on workshops, talks, and events-both online and in-person. Grow your skills, network, and career with GDG.
